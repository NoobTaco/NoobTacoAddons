Overview

Welcome to the curated guide for essential World of Warcraft addons. This application provides a clear, organized list of recommended addons for various high-end content like raiding, Mythic+, and delves. The goal is to help you streamline your UI and improve your performance by providing the right information at the right time. Use the navigation on the left to jump to a specific category.

Essential Addons

This section covers the foundational addons that are either mandatory for a smooth experience or highly recommended for the significant quality-of-life improvements they provide. A solid base of addons is key to handling content effectively.

GTFO

Must-Have

The simplest and most vital addon on this list. GTFO provides an immediate, loud sound alert whenever you're standing in an area that will harm you. This is an essential tool for avoiding damage and staying alive in all types of content.

Plater Nameplates

Recommended

An extremely powerful and customizable addon that overhauls the default nameplates. With a good profile, it can show crucial information about enemies, such as casting interrupts, active debuffs, and priority targets directly on their nameplate.

Encounter Helpers

For any group content, knowing what abilities bosses and enemies are about to use is critical. These addons provide timers, alerts, and announcements to help you and your group coordinate and react to mechanics properly. You can choose either BigWigs or DBM as your primary boss mod.

BigWigs & LittleWigs

This pair of addons provides clean, customizable boss encounter timers and warnings. BigWigs is for raids, while LittleWigs handles dungeons and delves. They are known for being lightweight and efficient. Many players prefer these over alternatives like DBM.

Get BigWigs (Raids) ➔ Get LittleWigs (Dungeons) ➔

Voice Packs for BigWigs

These optional addons add clear voice alerts to BigWigs, providing audible warnings for important mechanics. This allows you to focus on the action without having to read every on-screen alert.

Neural Voice Pack ➔ Countdown Voice Pack ➔

DBM Event Announcement

A simple but very effective addon that announces in chat when an important ability or mechanic is targeting you. This helps your group react accordingly. It is compatible with a BigWigs setup, so you can use it even if you don't use DBM as your main boss mod.

WeakAuras

WeakAuras is the most powerful and flexible addon in the game. It allows you to import or create custom displays to track anything from ability cooldowns to complex boss mechanics. Below are the base addon and a recommended set of auras for current content.

How to Use WeakAuras

  1. First, install the main WeakAuras 2 addon and the required CauseseDB companion addon.
  2. In-game, type /wa to open the WeakAuras panel.
  3. Click the "Import" button on the top right.
  4. Click one of the "View on Wago.io" links below to open the page, then click the "Copy Import String" button on the website.
  5. Paste the string into the import window in-game and follow the prompts.
